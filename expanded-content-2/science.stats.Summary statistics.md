Let $X_1,X2, ..., X_n$ be random variables. 
Then the sample mean is defined as follows:

$\bar{X} = \frac{1}{n}\Sigma_{i=1..n}X_i$

The sample variance is defined as follows:
$S^2 = \frac{1}{n-1}\Sigma_{i=1..n}(X_i-\bar{X})^2$
